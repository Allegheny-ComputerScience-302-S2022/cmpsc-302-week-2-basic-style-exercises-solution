Applesauce Jumbles

Description

Mom's Applesauce Jumble cookies are super soft and delicious and are sure to be a hit at any event! A holiday classic in our family!

  • TypeCookies
  • Prep20 min
  • Cook10 min
  • Total30 min
  • Yieldsabout 4-5 dozen

Ingredients

Cookie

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ cup applesauce
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Browned Butter Glaze:

  • ⅓ cup butter or margarine
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla
  • 2-4 tablespoons hot water

Directions

  1. Mix all cookie ingredients in a bowl.
    • If dough is soft, cover and refrigerate.
  2. Heat oven to 375 degrees, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto un-greased cookie sheet.
  3. Bake until almost no indentation remains when touched, about 10 minutes.
  4. Immediately remove from cookie sheet.
  5. Cool; spread with brown butter glaze.

Brown Butter Glaze:

  1. Heat butter or margarine over low heat until golden brown; remove from heat.
  2. Stir in powdered sugar and vanilla.
  3. Beat in hot water until smooth and of desired consistency.
